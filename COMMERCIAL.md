# Commercial Licensing for Astrum Verum

Astrum Verum is a dual-licensed project. This model allows the software to be used for free under an open-source license while providing a path for proprietary commercial use.

## 1. Open Source Use (AGPL-3.0)

For most users, the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0** is the appropriate path. This is a "strong copyleft" license that is ideal for:
- Open-source projects.
- Academic research and education.
- Public non-commercial use.

**Important:** If you use this software to provide a service over a network (SaaS), the AGPL requires you to make your entire source code available to your users.

## 2. Commercial & Proprietary Use

If the AGPL-3.0 requirements are not compatible with your business model (e.g., you cannot or do not want to share your proprietary source code), we offer a **Commercial License**.

### Benefits of a Commercial License:
- **Proprietary Integration:** Use Astrum Verum in your closed-source software or SaaS products.
- **No Copyleft Obligations:** You are not required to release your source code.
- **Enterprise Support:** Options for priority bug fixes, architectural consulting, and custom integration assistance.
- **Legal Peace of Mind:** Professional indemnity and clear chain of ownership.
